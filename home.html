Welcome to Defense : The Ultimate Web Development Course

This website is my project submission for the Coder Army HTML-only Hackathon with the intent of showcasing my understnading in the semantic structures of HTML.

It serves as a personal, structured collection of my notes and summaries for Rohit Negi's incredible "Defense Course" on YouTube.

This hub will follow the entire journey to becoming a full-stack developer, covering the three main parts of the course:

  • Frontend : Starting with the ultimate trio of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and later moving to libraries like React.
  • Backend : Exploring technologies like Node.js and Express.js.
  • Database : Understanding and implementing databases like MongoDB.

The content here is my own honest interpretation of the lectures, designed to be a precise and helpful resource for revision. The notes will unfold one by one as the course proceeds.
